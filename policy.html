Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2025 Last Updated: May 15, 2025

SeaHistoryChecks ("we", "us", or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website www.seahistorychecks.com (the "Site") and services, including when you request a boat history report by submitting a HIN/HULL (HULL Identification Number).

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, and any other details you provide voluntarily.
  • Boat Information: HIN/HULL (HULL Identification Number) submitted for report generation.
  • Payment Information: Billing details, processed securely via our third-party payment processors (we do not store credit card information).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage data collected via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your order and deliver the requested boat history report via email.
  • Communicate with you about your order, including confirmations and support.
  • Improve our services, website functionality, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and Conditions.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in delivering our services (e.g., payment processors, email service providers).
  • Legal Authorities: If required to comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our assets.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, including for legal, tax, or accounting requirements.
