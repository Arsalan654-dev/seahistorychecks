Our Reports

What's Included in Our Reports?

Each SeaHistoryChecks report is meticulously compiled to provide all essential details you need about a vessel.

Accident

Our report details any accidents involving the vessel, including collisions, groundings, and fires, offering insights into the circumstances and outcomes that may affect the ship's condition or value.

Damage

Get information about any damages a ship has sustained, including structural, mechanical, or cosmetic issues, and their impact on the vessel's performance and market value.

Total Loss & Salvage

We provide details on whether the ship was declared a total loss, including salvage records, to help you understand the extent of damage and any repairs or claims.

Hurricane Damage

Reports highlight any hurricane-related damage the vessel has experienced, detailing repairs, insurance claims, and long-term effects on the ship's safety and structural integrity.

Theft

If the vessel was involved in theft or piracy, our report outlines the incident, including stolen goods or parts, legal proceedings, and any subsequent recovery or claims.

Recall

Learn if the ship has ever been subject to recalls, including the reasons for the recall, what was affected, and how it impacted the vessel's safety, compliance, and operational status.
